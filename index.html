TFI Baagundaali

Telugu cinema vaipu prapancham antha thirigi choodaale

"Cinema is Love!"

Super Star Mahesh Babu

"Tippi kodithe okkokka cinema ne chestaam yadadhiki. Andari cinemalu aadithe industry inka baaguntundi. Memu Memu baane untaam, meru mere inka baagovaali... baagundaali."

Rebel Star Prabhas

"Cinemalu theatre lo ne choodaali. Yendukante mana intlo pooja gadhi undhi ani gudiki velladam maaneyam ga."

Mega Star Chiranjeevi

"Baiti vallaki manam lokuva kaavoddhu. Road meedha padi manam allari cheskovoddhu. We all are one. Maa heros, maa artists, Cinna pedda comedy andharu kalistene mana industry. Mana telugu cinema."

Power Star Pawan Kalyan

"Dayachesi cinema ni champoddu! Art is the most beautiful thing, it unites us. It doesn't divide us."

Icon Star Allu Arjun

"Gelichina roju yevadaina gelavocchu, odipoyyina roju aagipovocchu. Gelichina odipoyyina unstoppable ga undaali, aade gelustaadu."

Man of Masses Jr NTR

"Gelupuni panchukovadaniki, otamini panchukovadaniki manushulu undali. Appude gelupu rettimpu avutundi, otami sagam avutundi."

Natural Star Nani

“I am a Telugu boy and I want to tell stories in Telugu, which can be watched by Tamil audiences, Malayalam audiences, Hindi audiences so that I can take my language and go to all the states in the country.”

Mass Maharaaja Ravi Teja

"Rey, suppose cinema poyindhi. Intikellipothaama? Cinema superhit ayyindi, cinema tiyyadam maanestaama? Cinema hit ayyina inko cinema tiyyaali, flop ayyina inko cinema tiyyaali. Manaki telisindi okkate, Cinema Cinema Cinema!"
